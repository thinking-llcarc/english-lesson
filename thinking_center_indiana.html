In areas that adopt DST, the clock is moved one hour forward for the summer. Thus, the local time varies across different areas of the country, depending on which time zone the area is in and whether DST is adopted.

2The state of Indiana, shown in the map below, is known for its 32. For decades, both the Eastern Time Zone and Central Time Zone have existed within the state. Many areas in the state have switched back and forth between Eastern Time and Central Time. Furthermore, until very recently, policy concerning DST differed from one place to another. Most areas in the Eastern Time Zone did not adopt DST, and the time stayed the same throughout the year; while in other areas, where DST was adopted, the clock was moved forward by one hour for the summer. In 2006, however, the entire state started to observe DST.

3Some have argued that the time system in Indiana 33. For example, a company in Indiana once scheduled a phone conference with companies in other states. When the meeting started, only half of the out-of-state participants were on the line. This was because the participants who missed the conference did not know in which time zone the company was located.

It is not easy to resolve this issue because being in a particular time zone has important consequences for Indiana residents. Local businesses may benefit from being in the Eastern Time Zone where the financial center of the entire country, Wall Street in New York City, is located. Meanwhile, this may not be convenient for residents in the western parts of Indiana who commute to major cities in the Central Time Zone, like Chicago, just across the state border.

4The controversy over time in Indiana was partly resolved in 2006, when the entire state started to observe DST. 34 This debate may continue as long as people living in different parts of the state have different needs.

段落1 — アメリカの時間帯の基本
The United States is a vast country.
アメリカ合衆国は広大な国である。
From the East Coast to the West Coast, there are four different time zones.
東海岸から西海岸にかけて、4つの異なる時間帯がある。
In addition, many parts of the country observe daylight saving time (DST) as well.
さらに、国内の多くの地域はサマータイム（DST）も採用している。
In areas that adopt DST, the clock is moved one hour forward for the summer.
DSTを採用している地域では、夏の間は時計が1時間進められる。
Thus, the local time varies across different areas of the country, depending on which time zone the area is in and whether DST is adopted.
したがって、現地時間はその地域がどのタイムゾーンにあるか、DSTが採用されているかどうかによって、国内の異なる地域にわたって変化する。
段落2 — インディアナ州の複雑な時間帯（問32）
The state of Indiana, shown in the map below, is known for its ① complex time system that has been changed many times over the years.
下の地図に示されているインディアナ州は、その長年にわたって何度も変更されてきた複雑な時間システムで知られている。
For decades, both the Eastern Time Zone and Central Time Zone have existed within the state.
何十年もの間、東部タイムゾーンと中部タイムゾーンの両方が州内に存在してきた。
Many areas in the state have switched back and forth between Eastern Time and Central Time.
州内の多くの地域が東部時間と中部時間の間を行き来してきた。
Furthermore, until very recently, policy concerning DST differed from one place to another.
さらに、ごく最近まで、DSTに関する方針は場所によって異なっていた。
Most areas in the Eastern Time Zone did not adopt DST, and the time stayed the same throughout the year; while in other areas, where DST was adopted, the clock was moved forward by one hour for the summer.
東部タイムゾーンのほとんどの地域はDSTを採用せず、時刻は1年中同じままだった。一方、DSTが採用されていた他の地域では、夏の間は時計が1時間進められた。
In 2006, however, the entire state started to observe DST.
しかし2006年に、州全体がDSTを採用し始めた。
段落3 — 問題の主張と解決の難しさ（問33）
Some have argued that the time system in Indiana ② harms local businesses working with companies outside of Indiana.
インディアナ州の時間システムはインディアナ州外の企業と仕事をする地元企業に害を与えていると主張する人もいる。
For example, a company in Indiana once scheduled a phone conference with companies in other states.
例えば、インディアナ州のある企業が、他の州の企業との電話会議を予定したことがあった。
When the meeting started, only half of the out-of-state participants were on the line.
会議が始まったとき、州外の参加者の半分しか回線につながっていなかった。
This was because the participants who missed the conference did not know in which time zone the company was located.
これは、会議に参加できなかった人たちが、その企業がどのタイムゾーンにあるかを知らなかったためだった。
It is not easy to resolve this issue because being in a particular time zone has important consequences for Indiana residents.
特定のタイムゾーンにいることはインディアナ州の住民にとって重要な影響をもたらすため、この問題を解決するのは容易ではない。
Local businesses may benefit from being in the Eastern Time Zone where the financial center of the entire country, Wall Street in New York City, is located.
地元企業は、国全体の金融センターであるニューヨーク市のウォール街が位置する東部タイムゾーンにいることで恩恵を受けるかもしれない。
Meanwhile, this may not be convenient for residents in the western parts of Indiana who commute to major cities in the Central Time Zone, like Chicago, just across the state border.
一方で、これは州境を越えたすぐのところにあるシカゴなど、中部タイムゾーンの主要都市に通勤するインディアナ州西部の住民にとっては、便利ではないかもしれない。
段落4 — 部分的な解決と残る議論（問34）
The controversy over time in Indiana was partly resolved in 2006, when the entire state started to observe DST.
インディアナ州の時間をめぐる論争は2006年に部分的に解決され、その年に州全体がDSTを採用し始めた。
② However, there is still disagreement about having two different time zones in the state.
しかし、州内に2つの異なるタイムゾーンを持つことについては、依然として意見の不一致がある。
This debate may continue as long as people living in different parts of the state have different needs.
州の異なる地域に住む人々が異なるニーズを持ち続ける限り、この議論は続くかもしれない。
